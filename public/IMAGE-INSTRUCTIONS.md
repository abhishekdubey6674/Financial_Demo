# Image Instructions

## Required Images

Place these images in the `public` folder:

### Hero Carousel (3 images)

1. **`slide1.jpg`** - Financial Advisor theme
2. **`slide2.jpg`** - Investment Planning theme  
3. **`slide3.jpg`** - Business Consulting theme

### About Section (2 images)

1. **`about1.jpg`** - Team collaboration (people working together on laptop)
2. **`about2.jpg`** - Business meeting (professional meeting in office)

### Why Choose Us Section (1 image)

1. **`consultant.jpg`** - Professional consultant with tablet/phone (business person in suit)

## Image Specifications

- **Recommended Size**: 1920x1080px or higher
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Content**: Office/business environment with professionals working
- **Style**: Modern, bright, professional atmosphere with natural lighting
- **Key Elements**: 
  - Professionals working (positioned on right side for text space on left)
  - Office plants/greenery
  - Modern office interior
  - Soft, natural lighting
  - Laptop/technology visible

## Suggested Themes for Each Slide

**Slide 1 (Financial Advisor):**
- Professional consultant meeting with client
- Office desk with financial documents
- Warm, trustworthy atmosphere

**Slide 2 (Investment Planning):**
- Business person analyzing charts/graphs
- Modern office with technology
- Professional, focused environment

**Slide 3 (Business Consulting):**
- Team collaboration or presentation
- Conference room or modern workspace
- Dynamic, professional setting
